Projects

Delta Robot

The delta robot is programmed to replicate any image provided as input by drawing it on a piece of paper. A workspace analysis was conducted, and a delta robot was designed using Matlab. The CAD model for the robot was created using Solidworks. Matlab was employed to perform tasks such as image processing, path planning, inverse kinematics, and visualization, with the assistance of Simscape.

Quadruped

The quadruped is a robotic system characterized by four legs. In order to analyze its pose and motion, inverse kinematics were simulated. The CAD model of the delta robot was initially designed using Solidworks. The robot's behavior was then simulated using Simulink Multibody. Matlab was utilized to perform comprehensive inverse kinematics calculations for the robot from the ground up.

Anamorphic Sculptures 

Anamorphic sculptures are three-dimensional artworks designed to create distorted or exaggerated perspectives when viewed from certain angles. Using an optimized algorithm, a simulated anamorphic sculpture was created, featuring the word "RAJA" when viewed from one angle and a simple eye logo from another angle. The algorithm was specifically designed to minimize any distortions or discrepancies in the transition between the two images

Kinetic Rain Installation

Kinetic Rain Installation refers to a type of artwork or installation that incorporates suspended particles, to create a visually dynamic and captivating display. Simulated visually appealing patterns and waves for control inputs in a Kinetic Rain installation.

Pose Estimation

Explored the pipeline of a stereo system utilized to determine the pose of a portable CMM, also known as HandyProbe, which is employed for measuring object geometry through probing. It begins with a brief introduction to stereo vision, followed by the importance of camera calibration. The pipeline includes fiducial markers and a marker detection algorithm, as well as a stereo matching algorithm and pose estimation methods, along with an analysis of associated challenges.

CAD models

CAD models of various projects were created, including the recreation of a Colt pistol, a delta robot, a phone case cover, and other walking mechanisms, among other enjoyable endeavors.

TurtleSim ROS

The Turtlesim in ROS was utilized for a series of tasks, including implementing PID control, managing acceleration and deceleration limits, and incorporating a two turtle chase scenario.
